**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Rule-Based Expert System:** The  service uses a deterministic, rule-based engine (if-then logic) with confidence scoring to provide explainable recommendations, avoiding black box AI in its initial version.
-   **Phased Implementation:** Breaking down large features (like the Smart Budget Optimizer) into distinct, testable phases (V1: Recommendations, V2: Timeline UI, V3: Automation) as guided by the user.
-   **Event Sourcing for Learning:** The  service logs every recommendation and outcome, creating a historical dataset that will be used to improve the recommendation engine over time.
-   **Modular Routing:** Using FastAPI routers () to organize endpoints by feature, keeping  cleaner.

-   **products**: The core collection, enriched with dozens of data signals, scores, and metadata.
-   **outcomes**: Tracks the long-term success of products launched by users.
-   **ad_tests**: Stores A/B test variations and their performance metrics.
-   **optimization_events**: (New) Logs every recommendation made by the budget optimizer, the data snapshot at the time, and the eventual outcome. {test_id, variation_id, timestamp, metrics_snapshot, recommendation_action, confidence}
