## 3. Trending Products

**Where:** Public page at `/trending-products`

Browse all products TrendScout has detected as trending in the UK market.

### How to use it
- **Browse** the product grid — each card shows the product image, name, category, launch score, and trend stage
- **Filter** by category using the filter bar at the top
- **Sort** by score, margin, or trend stage
- **Set a minimum margin** to only show profitable products
- **Search** using the search bar

### Understanding the cards
| Element | What it means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Launch Score** (0-100) | How strong this product opportunity is. 80+ is excellent. |
| **Trend Stage** | Where the trend is: Emerging, Rising, Exploding, Stable, or Declining |
| **Margin** | Estimated profit margin as a percentage |
| **Category** | Product category (e.g. Home & Kitchen, Beauty, Tech) |

### Comparing products
See **Section 4** below.

## 4. Product Comparison Tool

**Where:** Select products on `/trending-products`, then click **Compare Now** — or go to `/compare`

Compare 2-4 products side by side to decide which one to launch.

### How to use it

1. **Go to Trending Products** (`/trending-products`)
2. **Hover over a product card** — you'll see a small checkbox in the top-right corner
3. **Click the checkbox** on 2, 3, or 4 products you want to compare
4. A **floating bar** appears at the bottom of the screen showing your selected products
5. Click **"Compare Now"**
6. You'll see a full comparison table

### What the comparison shows

| Section | Metrics |
|---------|---------|
| **Performance Scores** | Launch Score, Demand Score, Competition Score, UK Search Volume |
| **Financial Metrics** | Retail Price, Supplier Cost, Margin % |
| **Trend & Growth** | Trend Stage, Growth Rate, TikTok Views, Competition Level |
| **Score Comparison** | Visual bar charts comparing Launch Score and Margin side by side |

The **best value** in each row is highlighted in blue.

### Saving & Sharing
- Click **"Save & Share"** to create a permanent link
- Click **"Copy Link"** to copy it to your clipboard
- Share the link with your team — **they don't need an account** to view it
- Shared comparisons live at `trendscout.click/compare/XXXXXXXX`

### Managing comparisons
- View all your saved comparisons at `/compare` (when logged in)
- Delete any comparison you no longer need

## 16. Synced Products

**Where:** Sidebar > **Synced Products** (`/synced-products`)

A unified view of all products from your connected stores (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, etc.).

### Features
- **Platform filter cards** — click to filter by Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, etc.
- **Search** — find products across all platforms
- **Sort** — by recently synced, price, or stock level
- **Per-platform sync buttons** — manually trigger a sync for any platform

### Auto-Sync
Your connected stores automatically sync every **6 hours**. You don't need to do anything — products stay up to date.

### Sync History
The **Sync Activity** panel shows:
- Last sync time per platform
- Number of products synced
- Error rates
- Click to expand the full sync log (shows manual vs scheduled syncs)

### No products showing?
If you see "No Synced Products", you need to connect a store first. Click **"Connect a Store"** to open the Connection Wizard.

## 19. Connection Wizard

**Where:** Click **"Connect Platform"** on the Connections page

A step-by-step guided flow for connecting any platform.

### Steps
1. **Choose Platform** — pick from the grid of available platforms (grouped by type)
2. **Setup Guide** — instructions on getting your API keys, with a link to the developer portal and your redirect URI (auto-filled, just copy it)
3. **Enter Credentials** — paste your API key, secret, or shop domain
4. **Connect** — review and confirm. For OAuth platforms, you'll be redirected to authorize.

### One-Click vs Manual
- **One-click** (e.g. Shopify when configured) — you just enter your shop domain and authorize. No API keys needed.
- **Manual** — you enter API credentials yourself. The wizard tells you exactly where to find them.

### Admin OAuth Credentials Panel
On the **Connections** page (`/settings/connections`), admins see an extra section: **"Admin: OAuth App Credentials"**.

This is where you configure platform-level OAuth app credentials so users get **one-click** connections.

**How to use it:**
1. Click **"Admin: OAuth App Credentials"** to expand
2. For each platform, click **"Configure"**
3. Enter the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** from the platform's developer portal
4. Click **"Save"**
5. The platform will now show as **"Configured"** and users can connect with one click

See **Section 28** for detailed setup instructions per platform.

## 29. FAQs & Troubleshooting

### General

**Q: How often is product data updated?**
A: The database is refreshed continuously. Trending products are recalculated daily. Connected stores auto-sync every 6 hours.

**Q: Is TrendScout UK-only?**
A: Yes, TrendScout focuses specifically on the UK market. All data — search volumes, competition levels, pricing — is UK-specific.

**Q: What data sources does TrendScout use?**
A: Amazon UK Movers & Shakers, TikTok trending data, Google Trends UK, Shopify store analysis, and proprietary AI scoring.

### Connections

**Q: My store says "expired" — what do I do?**
A: Go to Connections, find your store, and click **"Reconnect"**. OAuth tokens expire periodically and need to be refreshed.

**Q: I connected my store but no products appear**
A: Click the **Sync** button next to your store on the Synced Products page. If it still doesn't work, check the Sync History for error messages.

**Q: "OAuth redirect URI mismatch" error**
A: The redirect URI in your developer portal must exactly match the one in the table above. No trailing slashes, must be https.

### Comparisons

**Q: Can I share a comparison without an account?**
A: You need an account to *create* a comparison, but the shared link works for anyone — no account needed to *view* it.

**Q: How many products can I compare?**
A: Between 2 and 4 products at a time.

### Admin

**Q: Where do I enter platform OAuth credentials?**
A: Settings > Connections > expand "Admin: OAuth App Credentials" (visible to admin users only).

**Q: I saved credentials but the platform still shows "Not set"**
A: Try removing and re-adding the credentials. The in-memory cache refreshes automatically when you save.
